# Portfolio Introduction

This tab contains the technical writing portfolio of **Tom Patterson**. You can use the menus and links in the **left-hand pane** to navigate between available samples of my work, or click the buttons below to navigate to a particular category.

The portfolio samples on this site are sanitized excerpts of actual deliverables that I have created in past roles. Where applicable, these samples have been rewritten for security and privacy purposes to remove any sensitive or identifying product and company information. For example, in my *API Documentation* samples, all endpoint, parameter, and attribute names are fictional.

In a broader sense, this entire web site is a portfolio sample demonstrating my familiarity with the Docusaurus static site generator. I created this site by modifying the classic Docusaurus template to suit my own needs and preferences.

If you would like to see other samples of my work or wish to connect, please feel free to reach out to me on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/tompattersonTW).
